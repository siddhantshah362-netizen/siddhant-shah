Side A: The Association

CIMA is a not-for-profit organization that has been the backbone of the Canadian Independent Music Industry for half a century.

Operating as the biggest advocating body, CIMA aggressively champions the renewal of the Canadian Music Fund to the government every year, ensuring the survival and growth of independent art.

Side B: Global Export

Acting as the official trade office for the entire independent industry, CIMA sets up Canada House at major conferences around the world to export domestic talent.

Year-round, the association programs vital PD (Professional Development) sessions and high-level B2B events to enhance industry infrastructure—most notably, Make It Music, CIMA’s massive yearly conference.
